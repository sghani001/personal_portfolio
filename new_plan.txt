He repeats one line across the site as an identity anchor:

> "I don't just build APIs. I engineer systems that remain reliable, scalable, observable and maintainable."

We need our own version of this — a single sentence that we repeat in the hero, in a pull-quote block mid-page, and possibly in the footer. It should reflect what actually makes our work distinct: **ownership of full systems end-to-end, not just features**, plus the fact that we've handled money (payments/billing) and multi-tenant data, where mistakes are unrecoverable.

Draft options (pick/refine one):

- *"I don't just ship features. I own systems — from schema to billing to production — where zero data loss isn't a goal, it's a requirement."*
- *"Anyone can call an API. I build the multi-tenant systems, billing pipelines, and integrations that can't afford to be wrong."*
- *"I build for the parts of a product that can't fail quietly — auth, billing, and data isolation."*
- *"Not just full-stack. Full-ownership — from database schema to production billing to zero-downtime migration."*

This line should appear:
- In the hero as a sub-headline
- As a standalone pull-quote block (his "Engineering Philosophy" section, section 05)
- Possibly in the footer tagline
